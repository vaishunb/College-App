Students should dress appropriately.
  Cell phones are not allowed inside the campus.
  Students should help keep the college campus clean.
  Students should not smoke inside the campus.
  Students should wear their identity cards at all times.
  Ragging is strictly prohibited.

  Students must respect college premises, buildings and furniture.
